Scopes we can take off your plate

  • Stucco and facade restoration

Waterproofing

Concrete

Exterior Construction

Violation Expertise

When the project involves a city violation, we understand the closeout requirements

Rajco performs corrective work associated with:

  • DOT sidewalk violations
  • DOB ECB / OATH violations
  • HPD violations
  • Local Law 11 / FISP repair scopes

We coordinate the physical repairs and provide the documentation required for the GC, owner, architect, engineer, or filing professional to move the closeout forward. Filing, certification, and sign-off remain with the appropriate licensed professional — architect, engineer, QEWI, or filing agent; we handle the repair work and the supporting documentation they need.
