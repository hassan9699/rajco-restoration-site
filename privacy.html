What we collect

When you contact us by phone, email, our online booking calendar, or a lead form linked from one of our ads, we collect the information you provide directly — typically your name, phone number, email address, property address, and a description of the work you're asking about. We don't collect this information any other way.

How we use it

We use this information to respond to your inquiry, schedule an inspection or appointment, and provide an estimate for construction or restoration work. If you book an appointment through our online calendar, your name and contact details are used to confirm and manage that appointment.

What we don't do

We don't sell or rent your information to third parties. We don't use it for anything beyond responding to your inquiry and providing the service you asked about.

Third-party services

Our website uses Google's advertising and analytics tools (Google Ads conversion tracking) to understand how visitors reach our site and which pages lead to a call, email, or booking. Our appointment booking is handled through Google Calendar. These services may set cookies or process data under their own privacy policies.

If you have questions about this policy or want us to delete information you've provided, email info@rajcorestoration.com or call (917) 409-8273.
